Why Maintenance

Why Regular Fire Safety Maintenance Matters

Legal Compliance

Indian law mandates fire safety systems for all commercial buildings. Regular maintenance keeps you compliant with NBC Part 4 and local fire brigade regulations.

Life & Asset Protection

Properly maintained systems detect and suppress fires early, saving lives and minimizing property damage during emergencies.

Insurance Validity

Most property insurance policies require valid fire NOC and maintenance records. Non-compliance can void your coverage and claims.

Peace of Mind

Knowing your fire safety infrastructure is in top condition lets you focus on your business operations without worry.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions About Fire Safety Services

Which fire safety service do I need for my commercial building?

Every commercial building in India requires a comprehensive fire safety setup as per NBC Part 4. Typically you will need fire alarm systems, fire extinguishers, hydrant systems, and a valid fire NOC. Start with a fire safety audit to identify gaps, then proceed with installations and maintenance as recommended.

How often should fire extinguishers be serviced?

Fire extinguishers should undergo visual inspection monthly, maintenance checks annually, and hydrostatic testing every 5-10 years depending on the type. FireSeva contractors provide scheduled maintenance plans to ensure your extinguishers are always ready.

What is a Fire NOC and why is it important?

A Fire NOC (No Objection Certificate) is a mandatory document issued by the fire department certifying that a building complies with fire safety norms. It is required for building approval, insurance renewal, and annual compliance. FireSeva consultants provide end-to-end NOC assistance to help you obtain and renew your certificate smoothly.
