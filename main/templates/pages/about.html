About Us

India's Trusted Fire Safety Network

FireSeva connects businesses and homeowners with verified, certified fire safety contractors across India — making compliance simple and reliable.

Our Mission

To make fire safety accessible and hassle-free for every Indian business and home. We bridge the gap between certified contractors and those who need them, ensuring quality, transparency, and compliance at every step.

Our Story

Why We Built FireSeva

Fire safety in India is often overlooked until it's too late. Property owners struggle to find reliable contractors, while certified professionals struggle to reach customers. We built FireSeva to solve this — creating a transparent, verified marketplace where quality meets trust. Every contractor on our platform goes through document verification, service inspection, and background checks so you can hire with complete confidence.

Our Values

What Drives Us

Trust & Transparency

Every contractor is verified for credentials, insurance, and past performance before they can list on our platform.

Safety First

We prioritise compliance with Indian fire safety norms (NBC Part 4) and ensure all listed services meet regulatory standards.

Quality Assurance

All services are physically inspected for operational readiness and service quality before being recommended to clients.

Transparent Pricing

No hidden costs, no middlemen. We standardise service rates so you know exactly what you're paying for.

24/7 Support

Pan-India Reach
